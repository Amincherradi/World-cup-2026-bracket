Under the hood

Engineered to stay live and stay up

Not a mock-up. A working product with a real data pipeline and graceful fallbacks, already deployed.

01

Real-time data

Polls live fixtures on a fast cadence; running score and minute refresh without a reload.

02

Self-resolving bracket

Group standings, clinched spots, best-third placement and eliminations are computed from results — no manual upkeep.

03

Layered fallbacks

Premium feed → free feed → cached snapshot. The board always renders something correct, even if a source fails.

04

Predict engine

One tap projects the full knockout path from current form and real outcomes — built for engagement and shares.

05

Drag, tap, play

Drag on desktop, tap-to-place on phones, with a bracket that scales to fit any screen edge-to-edge.

06

Format-agnostic

The 48-team layout is data-driven, so the same engine adapts to other cups, leagues and seasons.
